📚 Educational Modules

Learn to stay safe, healthy, and prevent water-borne diseases in your community.

🧼 Proper Hand Washing

Learn how to wash hands correctly to prevent water-borne and contagious diseases.

Steps:
  • Wet hands with clean water
  • Apply soap and rub for 20 seconds
  • Scrub between fingers, under nails, and back of hands
  • Rinse thoroughly and dry with a clean towel
Actions Villagers Can Take:
  • Install hand-washing stations in homes and schools
  • Encourage children to wash hands before meals
  • Use ash or sanitizer if soap is unavailable
Health Tips:
  • Wash hands after using the toilet or cleaning surroundings
  • Teach community about hygiene importance
  • Prevent spread of diarrhea, cholera, typhoid
💧 Safe Drinking Water

Understand purification methods and avoid contamination at the household level.

Steps:
  • Boil water for at least 5 minutes
  • Use filters or purification tablets
  • Store water in clean, covered containers
Actions Villagers Can Take:
  • Regularly clean storage containers
  • Protect water sources from contamination
  • Report polluted water sources to local health officials
Health Tips:
  • Drink only treated water
  • Avoid using water from ponds or stagnant sources
  • Prevent diseases like cholera, typhoid, hepatitis A
🩺 Disease Prevention Tips

Hygiene, sanitation, and early detection tips to prevent water-borne illnesses.

Steps:
  • Keep surroundings clean and dispose of waste properly
  • Cook food thoroughly and drink safe water
  • Wash fruits and vegetables before consumption
Actions Villagers Can Take:
  • Educate family and neighbors about hygiene practices
  • Encourage reporting of symptoms early
  • Participate in community clean-up drives
Health Tips:
  • Recognize early signs of diarrhea, cholera, or typhoid
  • Visit health centers promptly
  • Encourage vaccination where available
🗑️ Sanitation & Waste Management

Learn how proper sanitation and waste disposal prevent water-borne and other infectious diseases.

Steps:
  • Dispose household waste in designated bins
  • Keep surroundings clean and avoid open defecation
  • Ensure drainage systems are not blocked
Actions Villagers Can Take:
  • Organize community clean-up drives
  • Educate neighbors about safe waste disposal
  • Maintain cleanliness around water sources
Health Tips:
  • Prevent diarrhea, cholera, and other infections
  • Encourage children to follow hygiene practices
  • Report clogged drains or sanitation issues to authorities
💡 Quick Tips & Alerts
  • ✅ Wash hands with soap before meals and after using the toilet
  • ✅ Drink only boiled or filtered water
  • ⚠️ Avoid stagnant water to prevent mosquito-borne diseases
  • ✅ Cover food and water containers to prevent contamination
  • ⚠️ Report any sudden diarrheal cases to the local health worker
  • ✅ Participate in community clean-up activities regularly
  • 💧 Ensure children follow hand-washing routines at school
  • ✅ Keep kitchens and food preparation areas clean
  • ⚠️ Avoid using untreated pond or river water
  • ✅ Encourage neighbors to practice safe water habits
  • 💉 Make sure vaccinations are up to date in your family
  • 🧹 Maintain proper drainage around homes to prevent mosquito breeding
  • 📢 Spread awareness about safe hygiene practices in the village
  • 🌱 Plant trees and maintain greenery to reduce dust and contamination
